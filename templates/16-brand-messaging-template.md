## Applying This Framework

### When writing any customer-facing content, ask:

1. **Value prop**: Does this communicate our value clearly?
2. **Audience**: Is this relevant to our target audience's pain points?
3. **Pillars**: Does this reinforce one of our messaging pillars?
4. **Voice**: Does this sound like us?
5. **Tone**: Is the tone appropriate for the context?
6. **Proof**: Do we back up claims with evidence?
7. **Clarity**: Can someone understand this in 5 seconds?
