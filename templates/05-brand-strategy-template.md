## Decision Framework

**Using this brand strategy**

When making decisions, ask:

1. **Does this align with our brand purpose?** [Why we exist]
2. **Does this reflect our core values?** [Especially: which value?]
3. **Does this match our personality?** [How we show up]
4. **Does this deliver on our promise?** [What customers can count on]
5. **Does this differentiate us?** [How we're unique]

If the answer to any question is "no," reconsider the decision.
