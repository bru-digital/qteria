## The Universal Problem-Solving Question

Before fixing ANYTHING in business, always ask:
**"Is this the CAUSE or the EFFECT?"**

The loud alarm = EFFECT
The obvious problem = EFFECT
The quick fix = BANDAID
The hidden root issue = CAUSE ‚úÖ

## 3 Lessons for Working with AI Tools

1. **The first problem you see is rarely the real problem** - Like a fever isn't the illness, it's a symptom

2. **AI needs context to help effectively** - Imagine asking a consultant to solve a problem but only showing them the last email. Give them the whole story.

3. **Document what doesn't work** - Your failed attempts become guardrails for the next person (or AI) trying to help

We transformed this crisis into a systematic approach: Always dig at least 4 layers deep.
