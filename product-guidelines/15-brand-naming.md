## Naming Strategy

### Direction

**Naming approach**: Abstract/Compound with professional European feel

**Reasoning**:
The brand personality is a **competent quality management professional** who is direct, serious, and authoritative but approachable. Abstract/compound names with Latin or European roots (like "Qteria" - Quality + Criteria) fit this personality better than:
- **Descriptive names** (ValidateFast, ComplianceCheck): Too generic, sound like commodity tools (violates differentiation)
- **Playful names** (bouncy syllables, missing vowels): Misaligned with serious compliance work
- **Purely invented names** (no meaning): Miss opportunity to connect to core value (criteria, quality, evidence)
